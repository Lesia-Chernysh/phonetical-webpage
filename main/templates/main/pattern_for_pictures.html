Vowels

[ɪ] vs [i]

It might be important to differentiate these two vowels, as in some cases they are the only sounds making a difference between two words. For example: дим-дім [d̪ɪm]-[d̪im] (smoke-house), лис-ліс [lɪs]-[lʲis] (fox-forest),злива-зліва ['zlɪva]-['zlʲiva] (heavy rain - on the left) ect. Both sounds belong to front vowels, and for a non-native speaker they might sound very similar. However, there are differences in articulation: during the pronunciation of [ɪ] the tongue is placed lower compared to [i]. Try yourself, using the pictures and the audios.
